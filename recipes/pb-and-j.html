Peanut butter and jelly

A simple, and delightful meal comprised of three simple ingredients. Goes great with a glass of milk.

Ingredients

  • Peanutbutter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Get bread: Set the bread on something. I usually use a plate or papertowel. You want two slices of bread.
  2. Put jelly on one side: Use a knife to spread the jelly onto one of the pieces of bread.
  3. Put the peanutbutter on the other slice of bread repeat the application process of the previous step.
  4. Put the two slices together: set one piece of bread ontop of the other, with the condiment covered sides touching in the middle, and the bare bread sides facing down and up respectivly.
  5. Enjoy!
